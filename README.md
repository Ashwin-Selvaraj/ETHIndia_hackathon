# **Creator Funding Portal**

**Objectives**
A decentralized platform for content creators to fund their content with new-age tech. The creators can create a profile and upload their content. Users can fund the creators and creators can also fund other creators.

**Problem it solves**
There are many underrated and unrecognized content creators who work really hard every single day without much recognition and rewards. With the existing social media platforms, these creators do not get enough for the work they put in.
